About PlantCare AI

Smart crop diagnostics powered by deep learning

🎯

Our Mission

Millions of small-scale farmers lose crops each year to preventable plant diseases. PlantCare AI brings state-of-the-art computer vision directly to smartphones, enabling instant, accurate diagnosis without needing an agronomist on-site.

🧠

Model Architecture

We use MobileNetV2 pretrained on ImageNet as a feature extractor combined with a custom classification head:

Input 224×224 RGB
↓
MobileNetV2 (frozen backbone)
↓
Global Average Pooling 2D
↓
Dropout 0.3
↓
Dense 256 (ReLU)
↓
Dropout 0.2
↓
Dense 38 (Softmax)
📈

Training Details

87,000+ Training Images
38 Disease Classes
95 %+ Validation Accuracy
Adam Optimizer (LR 1e-4)
224×224 Input Resolution
< 2 s Prediction Time
📚

Dataset

Trained on the PlantVillage / New Plant Diseases Dataset — a curated, publicly available collection of over 87,000 leaf images spanning 14 crop species and 38 classes (healthy + diseased).

Data augmentation during training included random rotations, flips, zoom, brightness variations, and shear transforms to ensure robustness to field conditions.
